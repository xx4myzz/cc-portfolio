an abstract map of manhattan

click on the days of the week to see where i spent my time each day (home not included)

reflection

The personal habit I was trying to track was my daily locations and recording which areas I frequented.

I drew an abstract map of manhattan and had circles to represent the areas I found myself. The size of the circles represents the amount of time I spent at the areas.

To begin my sketch, I first listed out the areas I was in each day and the amount of time I spent to organize the locations and help me visualize.

I learned that I spend a lot of time in areas not near where I live, but in areas that my friends were located in.
